About

MumbleArt is a way for small/independent artists/photographers to submit their work. Their work can then be used by web designers or whoever as stock photos or decorations for whatever they may need. All work must be submitted for review so as to keep the content high quality. See the submissions page for instructions.

FAQ

  • Who uses MumbleArt and why?
    • Mumble art is a way for people to get free quality stock art/images. For artists its exposure. For developers and others who need image its a free alternative to stock photos. Our mission is to make getting images simple and free. We want to create a community that is open and supportive of small projects.

  • I'm an artist will I get paid?
    • No you will not be. This is a website that helps you get your work to be seen by people.

  • How does this website stay up?
    • Advertisements help pay for the servers so don't use adblock!

  • Will Information about my work be posted?
    • Only what you want to be posted. We encourage everyone to use copyrights though.
